📊 Marketing Campaign Analysis & Correlation Heatmap
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign data to evaluate campaign performance, customer engagement, and return on investment (ROI). The goal is to explore relationships between different campaign metrics such as acquisition cost, engagement score, conversion rate, and ROI.

Using Python-based data analysis and visualization techniques, the project identifies patterns in campaign performance across various channels, customer segments, and locations.

This analysis helps demonstrate data-driven decision making in marketing analytics.

🎯 Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are:

Analyze the effectiveness of different marketing campaigns

Understand the relationship between acquisition cost and ROI

Identify high-performing marketing channels and campaign types

Explore customer segmentation patterns

Discover correlations between engagement and conversion metrics

📊 Dataset Description

The dataset contains information about marketing campaigns including:

Column	Description
Campaign_ID	Unique identifier for each campaign
Company	Company running the campaign
Campaign_Type	Type of marketing campaign
Channel_Used	Marketing channel used
Acquisition_Cost	Cost spent to acquire customers
Conversion_Rate	Percentage of conversions
Engagement_Score	Customer engagement metric
ROI	Return on Investment
Location	Campaign location
Customer_Segment	Target customer group
Target_Audience	Campaign target audience

🔎 Data Analysis Workflow
1️⃣ Data Loading

The dataset is loaded from an online source using Pandas and inspected for structure and data types.

2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Key steps performed:

Examined dataset structure and summary statistics

Analyzed campaign distribution by channel and type

Investigated customer segments and locations

3️⃣ Visualization Analysis

Several visualizations were created to explore campaign performance:

📈 Scatter Plot – Acquisition Cost vs ROI

📊 Bar Chart – Conversion rate by marketing channel and campaign type

📊 Profit Analysis – ROI comparison across companies

🔥 Correlation Heatmap – Relationship between engagement score and conversion rate

🥧 Pie Chart – ROI contribution by location

📊 Customer Segmentation Chart – Target audience distribution

📈 Key Insights

✔ Marketing channels show varying conversion rates depending on campaign type.

✔ Higher engagement scores often correlate with better conversion rates.

✔ ROI differs significantly across companies and campaign strategies.

✔ Location-based performance highlights markets contributing most to campaign success.
